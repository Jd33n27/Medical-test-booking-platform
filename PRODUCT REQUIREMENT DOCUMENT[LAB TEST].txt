Executive Summary
Accessing laboratory testing services in Nigeria is often inconvenient, time consuming, and lacks transparency. Patients typically have limited visibility into available laboratories, test pricing, appointment availability, and home sample collection services. Most diagnostic centers still require physical visits or phone calls before a test can be scheduled.
The Lab Test is a healthcare booking platform that enables patients to discover, compare, and book laboratory tests online. Users can search for tests, compare prices across accredited laboratories, schedule appointments, request home sample collection, make payments, and receive digital results.
The MVP focuses on simplifying the diagnostic testing journey while improving transparency, accessibility and convenience.
Product Vision
To become Nigeria's most trusted platform for discovering, comparing, and booking laboratory tests.
Problem Statement
Patients seeking laboratory testing services encounter several challenges:
* Limited visibility into available laboratories and diagnostic centers.
* Inconsistent and inaccessible pricing information.
* Long wait times and multiple hospital visits before scheduling tests.
* Difficulty finding laboratories offering home sample collection.
* Delayed access to laboratory results.
* Lack of a centralized platform for comparing available options.
These challenges increase friction, delay diagnosis, and reduce access to healthcare services.
Product Solution
The Lab Test provides a centralized platform where users can:
* Search for laboratory tests.
* Compare prices across laboratories.
* Discover nearby diagnostic centers.
* Schedule appointments online.
* Request home sample collection.
* Make secure online payments.
* Receive digital test results.
The platform connects patients with accredited laboratories while making the booking process faster and more transparent.
Product Overview
The Lab Test is a web based healthcare marketplace that aggregates diagnostic laboratories and testing services.
The platform allows users to:
* Search for specific tests.
* Compare laboratories based on price and location.
* Book appointments online.
* Request home sample collection.
* Access test results digitally.
The product prioritizes transparency, trust, and convenience.
